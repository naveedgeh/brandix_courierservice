What is Timed Delivery?

Timed delivery permits you to get and deliver parcels at a time to suit you and your recipient. Timed delivery permits you to know precisely when your parcels will be delivered, so there is no compelling reason to remain at home throughout the day. By picking a chance to suit you, you can be certain that you will get your parcels exactly on schedule.


What are the benefits of Timed Delivery?

Choose Time: With Timed Delivery service offered by Sprint, Freight Couriers allows you to send the package according to the time availability of the recipient.

Tracking Order: Sprint Freight Courier allows you to track your timed courier order. You can track your order and this will provide you peace of mind.

Low Shipping Costs: Our timed courier service will reduce the shipping costs for you and is less expensive than other standard shipping companies in the UK.

Pick Up Service: If you want to deliver your parcel on an urgent basis, contact us and we will pick the parcel from your doorstep and deliver it within a day with our timed courier service.
